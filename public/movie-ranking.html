The Best Animes According To Nitish

My Top 3 anime recommendation for starters

Naruto

Story about an orphaned boy who with sheer work & effort became the leader of his village (Hokage).

One Piece

Story about a boy who sails to the sea in the hunt of a treasure chest (ONE PIECE) and his journey to reaching it.

Blue Lock

Journey about a boy who wanted to play football for his country in the WORLD CUP and his journey to reaching it.
